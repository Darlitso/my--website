FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

How do I schedule a test drive?

Simply fill out the contact form above and select "Schedule a Test Drive" as your subject. Or call us directly at +27 11 234 5678 and we'll arrange a convenient time for you.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We partner with multiple banks and financial institutions to offer competitive financing rates. We also offer in-house financing plans tailored to your budget.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Absolutely. We accept trade-ins on all vehicle purchases. Bring your car to our showroom for a free on-the-spot valuation, or use our online trade-in calculator.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not 100% satisfied with your vehicle, return it within 7 days for a full refund — no questions asked.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes, we offer nationwide delivery across South Africa. Delivery fees vary based on distance. For international inquiries, please contact our sales team directly.

Are all vehicles inspected before sale?

Every vehicle goes through our rigorous 150-point inspection process before being listed. We also provide a full vehicle history report with every purchase.
